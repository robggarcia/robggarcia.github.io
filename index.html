About Me

Hello World! My name is Rob Garcia and I love to code! I am an Engineer, Educator, Musician, and Life-long Learner. I am completing courses through Codecademy, freeCodeCamp, and will soon begin pursuing an Undergraduate Certificate in Cloud Computing at the University of Virginia.

After teaching Phyiscs and Math in Philadelphia, my wife and I decided to move to Virginia in the summer of 2020 in order to be closer to family. I found a job as Engineering Teacher at Charlottesville High School where I teach students from 9th through 12th grade how to program microcrontrollers and use the OnShape CAD application software in order to 3D print and laser cut their designs. Within this past year, I have completely fallen in love with the programming and hope to see where it can take me.

In my free time I like to write and record music, build guitar amps from scratch, learn about signal processing, and enjoy the great outdoors. I'm 39 years old and I've been learning and teaching others to code for exactly milliseconds!
